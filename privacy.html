Privacy Policy for CuyahogaLookup

Last Updated: July 18, 2026

Short version: we don't collect your data. CuyahogaLookup has no ads, no accounts, no analytics, and no tracking. Everything you do in the app stays on your device. This matches our App Store privacy label of Data Not Collected.

Introduction

CuyahogaLookup ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles information. CuyahogaLookup is a free app that surfaces publicly available Cuyahoga County property records. It does not require an account and does not display advertisements.

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, transmit, sell, or store your personal information on any server. Specifically, the app does not:

  • Show advertisements or use any advertising SDK
  • Access or use your device's advertising identifier (IDFA), and it does not ask to track you
  • Include any third-party analytics, attribution, or tracking SDK
  • Create user accounts or require sign-in
  • Operate any server that stores your personal data

Information Stored On Your Device Only

The following is created and stored solely on your device to make the app work. It is never transmitted to us and we cannot see it:

  • Search history: recent searches, kept on-device for convenience.
  • Favorites & notes: properties you save and any personal notes you add.
  • Settings: your theme, filters, and preferences.

You can remove this data at any time from within the app, or by deleting the app.

Permissions You Control

  • Location (optional): used only, in the moment, to run a "near me" search and show your current street. Your location is used to build a search query and is not stored by us or transmitted anywhere except as part of that public property search request.
  • Contacts (optional): only when you tap "Add to Contacts," the app writes a property owner's name and address into your Contacts. The app does not read or upload your contacts wholesale; contact matching for the "Saved" view happens on your device.
  • Notifications (optional): used to alert you to changes on properties you have saved. These checks run on your device.

Property Data (Public Records)

Property information shown in the app — including owner names, addresses, values, taxes, and sales — comes directly from publicly available records published by the Cuyahoga County Fiscal Officer and related public county GIS services, and from OpenStreetMap for the map and business lookups. When you search, your query is sent to those public services to return results. We do not add tracking to those requests beyond a standard app identifier in the request header, and we do not store the results on any server of ours.

CuyahogaLookup is an independent app and is not affiliated with or endorsed by Cuyahoga County. The data is provided as a public service without warranty; always verify with the county for official information.

Data Storage and Security

  • All user data (searches, favorites, notes, settings) is stored locally on your device.
  • Favorites are stored in the device keychain so they survive reinstalls; other data uses standard on-device app storage.
  • We do not operate servers that store your personal data.
  • Requests to public property services are made over encrypted (HTTPS) connections.

Children's Privacy

CuyahogaLookup is not directed to children under 13, and because we do not collect personal information, we do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the new policy here and update the "Last Updated" date above.
