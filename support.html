CuyahogaLookup Support

CuyahogaLookup helps you search Cleveland & Cuyahoga County, Ohio property records — owners, home values, and parcel info for all 59 cities — by address, owner name, business name, or parcel ID.

Contact us

Questions, bugs, or feedback? Email justin@justreed.com and we'll get back to you.

Getting started

  • Search by address — type any street address (e.g. "Main Street").
  • Search by owner — type a person or company name (e.g. "Smith").
  • Search by parcel ID — enter the official parcel number.
  • Search by business name — find properties by the business located there.
  • Near me — use your location to find nearby properties.

Saving & organizing

  • Tap the heart to save a property to your Saved list.
  • Save an owner to your iOS Contacts (with the property address) right from the detail screen.
  • Sort and filter your saved list by distance, name, street, value, and more.

Premium

CuyahogaLookup Premium (an auto-renewing annual subscription) removes all advertisements. Subscriptions are billed through your Apple ID and can be managed or cancelled in Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions.

Data & privacy

Property data comes from the public Cuyahoga County Fiscal Officer records. Your searches and favorites stay on your device. See our Privacy Policy.
